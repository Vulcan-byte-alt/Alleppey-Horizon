Hotels & Resorts

Lake Palace

Lake Palace Alappuzha is a 5-star resort that offers you free Wi-Fi, cozy beds, and deluxe amenities for a comfortable stay. You can also enjoy a free breakfast, a fitness center, a swimming pool, and a spa. The resort has a private lagoon surrounded by backwaters, and overlooks the Vembanad Lake – the longest freshwater lake in India. You can also savor the exotic flavors of the coast and delectable global cuisines at the resort’s restaurant.

Abad Turtle Beach is a seaside resort in Mararikulam, Alleppey, that overlooks the white sandy beach and a rivulet. The resort has 30 premium cottages and villas, with free Wi-Fi, cozy beds, and deluxe amenities. You can also enjoy a free breakfast, a swimming pool, a spa, and an organic garden.

Raheem Residency

Raheem Residency is a heritage hotel at Alleppey Beach, dating back to 1868. It has 10 rooms with free Wi-Fi, cozy beds, and antique furniture. You can also enjoy a free breakfast, a swimming pool, a spa, and a yoga center.Additionally, you can savor the delicious cuisine at the hotel’s restaurant, which offers a sea view and a variety of dishes from local and international cuisines

Classic Regency

Classic Regency is a 4-star hotel in Alappuzha, also known as the Venice of the East, that offers you a splendid view of the beach and the city’s cultural heritage. The hotel has 30 well-appointed rooms, with free Wi-Fi, cozy beds, and deluxe amenities. You can also enjoy a free breakfast, a fitness center, a swimming pool, a spa, and an organic garden. The hotel’s restaurant serves a variety of mouth-watering cuisine, from Chinese, Indian, and local dishes. You can also relax at the coffee shop, or avail the travel desk services for flight reservations, cab for sightseeing, houseboat booking, and more. Classic Regency is a safe haven dedicated to your well-being and comfort, where you can experience the best of Kerala on a silver platter.

Zostel Aleppey

Zostel Alleppey is a 1-star resort that is a backpacker’s paradise. It is situated right at the scenic Alappuzha Beach, and is ideal for exploring the town and its backwaters. It has 12 rooms, with free Wi-Fi, cozy beds, and lockers. You can also enjoy a free breakfast, a common area, and a rooftop terrace. Moreover, you can join the hostel’s activities, such as boat tours, village walks, and cultural events, and meet other travellers from around the world. Zostel Alleppey is a lively and budget-friendly option that offers you a fun and memorable stay on the shores of the Arabian Sea.

Brooklands Avenue

Brooklands Avenue is a 1-star hotel that is located near the scenic Alappuzha Beach, and is ideal for exploring the town and its backwaters. The hotel has 30 rooms, with free Wi-Fi, cozy beds, and basic amenities. You can also enjoy a free breakfast, a garden, a shared lounge, and a terrace. The hotel provides room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free private parking for guests who drive. The hotel also has a restaurant that serves a variety of mouth-watering cuisine, from Chinese, Indian, and local dishes. You can also avail the travel desk services for flight reservations, cab for sightseeing, houseboat booking, and more. Additionally, you can relax at the hotel’s rooftop, where you can enjoy the panoramic view of the beach and the sunset. Brooklands Avenue is a budget-friendly option that offers you a comfortable and convenient stay on the shores of the Arabian Sea.
