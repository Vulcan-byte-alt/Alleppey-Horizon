Full Day Shikara Cruise through Alleppey Backwaters(8 hours)

Embark on an enchanting 8-hour Shikara Cruise, commencing from the serene Rajiv Boat Jetty in Alleppey. Glide through the backwaters, exploring the starting and finishing points of the illustrious Snake Boat Race, where the air is filled with tales of tradition and competition.
As you navigate the vast Vembanad Lake, the tranquility envelops you, creating a serene backdrop for your journey. The Sports Authority of India (SAI) stands as a symbol of athletic vigor along the route.
Traverse the narrow canals of C Block, immersing yourself in the local rhythm, and witness the innovative cultivation of paddy fields below sea level, showcasing the resilience of local agriculture.
The route is punctuated by ancient temples, offering a cultural interlude, while glimpses of duck farming and fish hatcheries provide a connection to the livelihoods of the community.
Your voyage culminates with a return to the Rajiv Jetty, leaving you with lasting memories of a day immersed in the beauty and heritage of Alleppey's backwaters.

Step into a 5-hour odyssey on a Shikara Ride to the tranquil Pathiramanal island in Alleppey.

Begin your journey with a gracious pick-up from the picturesque Rajiv Boat Jetty, setting the tone for a day of exploration. As you navigate the waterways, discover the essence of the world-famous Snake Boat Race at its starting, model, and finishing points, each narrating tales of tradition and sporting prowess.
Cruise across the expansive Vembanad Lake, where the calm waters mirror the surrounding beauty. Pass by the Sports Authority of India (SAI), embodying the vibrant spirit of the region.
Meander through narrow canals, immersing yourself in the local rhythm, and witness the innovation of paddy fields cultivated below sea level. Along the way, encounter ancient temples, each a testament to the cultural richness of the region.
The journey crescendos as you reach Pathiramanal island, a haven of tranquility amidst the backwaters. Explore its charm, and let the surroundings captivate your senses.
Finally, the Shikara gracefully returns to the Rajiv Jetty, concluding a 5-hour escapade that intertwines nature, culture, and the timeless allure of Alleppey's backwaters.
